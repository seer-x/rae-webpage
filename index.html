Benefits of Risk Aggregation Engine

Market Leader Approach

Based on aggregation of bottom-up loss scenario sets by line of business, following industry best practices.

Powerful Yet Simple

Aggregate loss and result distributions across LOBs using pricing loss scenario sets from vendor or internal tools.

Flexible Framework

Universal copula framework captures dependencies via hierarchical risk aggregation tree, reflecting your specific business mix.

Enhanced Reporting

Support regulatory, rating agency, and economic risk management reporting while enabling business steering decisions.

Capital Optimization

Apply retrocession structures to optimize capital efficiency and identify concentration risks.

Immediate Availability

Available as desktop application or web-based API with no upfront development costs.
